Bolognese

Description

Bolognese is an italian dish typically made using minced beef, tomatoes and spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Beef mince
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Dice the onions and garlic
  2. Brown the mince and onions in the pan
  3. Once brown add the garlic and cook for a further minute
  4. Add tinned tomatoes and cook 20-30 minutes until sauce has thickened
  5. Cook spaghetti in boiling water and serve alongside the bolognese
