Pizza

Description

Pizza is an italian dish which uses a bread base and the option of different toppings.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Basil
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Mozerella cheese

Steps

  1. Mix flour, yeast and sugar
  2. Knead for about 5-10 minutes
  3. leave dough for a few hours until risen
  4. Roll the dough out and add the tomatoes and mozerella
  5. Bake until browned
